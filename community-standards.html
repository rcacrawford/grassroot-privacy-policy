Grassroot Community Standards

Grassroot is committed to fostering respectful discussion while maintaining a safe environment for all users. Users are expected to engage responsibly and comply with these Community Standards.

Prohibited Content

Users may not post or share content that:

  • Contains or promotes child sexual abuse or exploitation (CSAM/CSAE).
  • Sexually exploits or endangers minors in any way.
  • Contains illegal content or encourages criminal activity.
  • Threatens, harasses, bullies, or intimidates other individuals.
  • Promotes violence against individuals or groups.
  • Encourages hate or discrimination based on protected characteristics.
  • Impersonates another person or organization in a misleading manner.
  • Contains spam, scams, or fraudulent activity.

Child Safety

Grassroot has zero tolerance for child sexual abuse or exploitation. Any content involving the exploitation or endangerment of minors is strictly prohibited and will be removed immediately when identified.

Accounts responsible for such content may be permanently suspended or terminated. Where required by law, Grassroot may cooperate with appropriate law enforcement authorities.

Reporting Violations

Users are encouraged to report content or accounts that violate these Community Standards.

Reports may be submitted by contacting:

bobbycrawford@grassrootsocial.com

Enforcement

Violations of these Community Standards may result in one or more of the following actions:

  • Removal of violating content.
  • Temporary account suspension.
  • Permanent account termination.
  • Referral to appropriate authorities when legally required.
