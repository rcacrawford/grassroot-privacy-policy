Privacy Policy for Grassroot Effective Date: July 21, 2026 Thank you for using Grassroot ("the App"). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to you. 1. Information We Collect When you use Grassroot, we may collect the following information: Account Information When you create an account, we may collect: Email address Username Display name Profile picture Password (handled securely through our authentication provider and never stored directly by Grassroot) User Content We collect and store content you choose to create, including: Posts Comments Replies Images Videos Likes Saved posts Follows We may collect limited technical information such as: Device type App version Crash reports 2. How We Use Your Information We use your information to: Create and manage your account Display your profile and content to other users Enable social features such as comments, follows, likes, and saved posts Store and deliver uploaded media Respond to support requests We do not sell your personal information. 3. Media Storage Photos and videos uploaded to Grassroot are stored using third-party cloud storage providers to allow fast and reliable delivery of media content. 4. However, no method of electronic storage or internet transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. 7. Your Choices You may: Update your profile information within the App. Delete content you have posted where the App provides that functionality. Request deletion of your account and associated personal information by contacting us. Some information may be retained as required by law or for legitimate business purposes, such as fraud prevention or legal compliance. 8. Children's Privacy Grassroot is not intended for children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child has provided personal information, we will take reasonable steps to remove that information. 9.
